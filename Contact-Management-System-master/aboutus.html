OUR HISTORY


Marwar Business School, established in 1999 by the Marwari Society Gorakhpur, in its long eventful journey has constantly striven to live up to its motto, by assimilating diverse ideas, people and beliefs into its academic life. Marwar Business School(A post graduate college affiliated to D.D.U. Gorakhpur University) is run by Shree Marwari Society, Gorakhpur. Shree Marwari Society Gorakhpur is a non profit making society registered in 1947. Our society run a Inter College named Marwar Inter College Gorakhpur. As a residential-cum-affiliating College it can boast of a rich academic legacy, illustrious alumni, experienced, qualified and dedicated faculty members, transparent, effective and responsive administrative set up, state-of-the art library, Wi-Fi campus, ample career growth opportunities for its students, advanced facilities and a vibrant and safe campus. To provide the quality oriented higher education in Gorakhpur, Marwar Business School was established in 1999. In 1999 affiliation was granted for B.com. classes and in 2005, B.Sc(Maths & Bio) classes were started. To upgrade the institution in 2012-13 post graduates in Commerce and Chemistry (M.Com and M.Sc) were started. Further we have granted affiliation for BBA, BCA and B.Sc.(Home Science) in the session 2013-14. The Science faculty functioning with a well-equipped laborataries in Zoology, Botany, Physics and Chemistry.
